Welcome to Teacup Puppies

Every year, countless puppies find themselves in need of a second chance due to neglect, abuse, or circumstance. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing a safe haven for these intelligent and complex puppies.

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome unwanted or neglected puppies, ensuring they receive the specialized care, veterinary attention, and compassion they deserve. Through education and advocacy, we strive to create a world where every puppy is safe, respected, and loved.
